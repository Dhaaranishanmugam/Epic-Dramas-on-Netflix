The Last Kingdom

Description:

Adapted from Bernard Cornwell's novels, "The Last Kingdom" follows Uhtred of Bebbanburg, a Saxon noble captured and raised by Vikings, as he seeks to reclaim his birthright amidst the backdrop of Alfred the Great's vision for a unified England.


Main Cast: Alexander Dreymon, David Dawson, Emily Cox, Ian Hart, Tobias Santelmann

Written by: Stephen Butchard

Directed by: Various (multiple directors)

Story:

"The Last Kingdom" is a historical drama filled with battles, alliances, and personal quests for identity and justice. Uhtred's journey explores themes of loyalty, cultural clash, and the price of power in a turbulent period of English history.


Release Date: 2015

Number of Episodes: 36 (across 5 seasons)

Peaky Blinders

Description:

Set in post-World War I Birmingham, England, this crime drama follows the Shelby crime family as they rise to power amidst economic instability, political tensions, and rival gangs.


Main Cast: Cillian Murphy, Helen McCrory, Paul Anderson, Joe Cole, Tom Hardy

Written by: Steven Knight

Directed by: Various (multiple directors)

Story:

"Peaky Blinders" portrays the Shelby family's ambitions and conflicts as they navigate the dangerous world of organized crime. With intricate plots, intense characters, and a gritty atmosphere, the series explores themes of loyalty, betrayal, and the impact of historical events on personal destinies.


Release Date: 2013

Number of Episodes: 30 (across 5 seasons)

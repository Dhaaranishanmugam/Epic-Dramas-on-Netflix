Dark

Description:

In a small German town, supernatural events expose a web of interconnected families and a time-travel conspiracy spanning multiple generations.


Main Cast: Louis Hofmann, Oliver Masucci, Karoline Eichhorn, Lisa Vicari, Andreas Pietschmann

Written by: Jantje Friese, Baran bo Odar

Story:

"Dark" is a mind-bending sci-fi thriller that explores the implications of time travel on human relationships and fate. As characters grapple with their intertwined past, present, and future, the series delves into themes of determinism, free will, and the haunting consequences of secrets kept across decades.
